HOW TO REACH NAGUEY?

You can now reach Naguey in 3 simple steps!

Step 1

If you are in Baguio Centermall, go to Dangwa Station just above the mall and look for the Atok bus. It is light green and white in color. You can easily find it since it is the only bus parking in between the Dangwa Station parking lot and Baguio Centermall.

Step 2

Upon finding it, load up. It usually leaves 11:00am to 12:00nn. It stops in Shilan, Acop for neccessities and for the passenger and driver to eat and shop. Just pay a fare of Php120.00 and you're now okay. Sitback, relax and enjoy the ride!


Step 3

The bus ends at Sitio Sawingan and it is also where the Barangay Hall is. For more inqueries and accommodation, you may asked their barangay captain. Then you can now freely roam the place!
